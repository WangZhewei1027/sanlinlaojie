# User Guide

Welcome to the AR Memory platform. This guide will help you get started.

## Getting Started

1. **Log in** to your account using your email and password.
2. From the **Home** page, choose the section you need.

## Viewing AR Content

- Go to **Manage** to browse all AR assets in your workspace.
- Click on any asset card to preview it.
- Use the **AR View** button to launch the augmented reality experience on your device.

## Uploading Assets

> This feature is available to editors and above.

1. Click **On-site Upload** from the home page.
2. Select your workspace.
3. Upload images, 3D models, audio, or video files.
4. Fill in the asset name and description, then click **Save**.

## Workspace

- All content is organized by **workspace**.
- Use the workspace switcher in the top navigation to switch between workspaces.
- Contact your administrator if you need access to a workspace.

## Account

- To update your password, go to **Settings → Update Password**.
- To log out, click the **Logout** button in the top navigation.

## Support

If you encounter any issues, please contact your organization administrator.
